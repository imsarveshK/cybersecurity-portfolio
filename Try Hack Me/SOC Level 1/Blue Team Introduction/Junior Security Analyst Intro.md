# Jr Security Analyst Intro

## Objective
Understand the role and responsibilities of a Junior Security Analyst within a Security Operations Center (SOC), including monitoring, investigation, and incident response processes.

---

## Concepts Covered
- Security Operations Center (SOC)
- Threat monitoring
- Alert investigation
- Incident response lifecycle
- Threat Intelligence
- Digital Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR)

---

## SOC Team Responsibilities

### Monitoring & Detection
A Security Analyst is responsible for:
- Monitoring security events and alerts
- Identifying suspicious activity
- Investigating potential threats
- Escalating confirmed incidents

---

## Incident Response Process

### Key Stages
- Preparation
- Identification
- Containment
- Eradication
- Recovery
- Lessons Learned

---

## Threat Intelligence

Learned how Threat Intelligence helps analysts:
- Identify Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
- Understand attacker behavior
- Improve threat detection capabilities

Examples of IOCs:
- Malicious IP addresses
- Suspicious domains
- File hashes
